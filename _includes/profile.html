I'm Fatemeh. I have PhD in Mechanical Engineering (Robotics) with 10+ years of extensive research experience on impedance/ force control, robot kinematics and dynamics, advanced control theories, robotic manipulation, optimization and estimation, mathematical modeling, machine learning, and statistical analysis. I have advanced knowledge in designing and implementing Human in the Loop optimization algorithms, as well as developing user adaptive variable impedance controls based on human bio-mechanics. I have gained hands-on experience working with various robots, sensors and platforms and have successfully integrated software and hardware components.

My main interests are robotics, control, impedance/force control, physical human-robot interaction, robot manipulation, and machine learning. I have also contributed to multiple publications and presentations in the field of robotics and control. I am passionate about developing innovative and effective solutions that enhance human-robot collaboration and improve human well-being.

Skill set: Robotics; Impedance/force control; Mathematical modeling; Advanced Control; Numerical methods and Optimization; Bayesian Optimization; Gaussian Processes; Machine learning libraries (scikit learn, GPy, Keras, PyTorch, etc.); Coding in Python, C++, MATLAB; Integration of software and hardware; Hand on experience with robots; Solution and implementation skills; Problem Identification; Presentation skills; Conflict management and handling problems; Analyzing and contributing results; Leadership and teamwork.
